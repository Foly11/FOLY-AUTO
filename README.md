# FOLY AUTO 🧭
### The Ultimate Premium Watch Faces Platform for Android Auto

FOLY AUTO is a cutting-edge, high-performance companion application engineered to project mathematical, ultra-high-fidelity analog clock layouts directly onto Android Auto head units. Fusing a sleek, iOS-inspired mobile control panel with a deeply optimized dynamic rendering engine, it brings a premium dashboard aesthetic inspired by luxury horology and industrial design.

## 🌟 Core Features

### 1️⃣ 26 Premium Watch Faces (Vector Procedural Rendering)
Every single watch face is drawn dynamically using custom mathematical paths and canvas coordinates instead of heavy, lag-inducing static assets.
- **Luxury & Motorsports:** Inspired by timeless Swiss chronographs, featuring the legendary *Omega Speedmaster Pro* with functioning sub-dials, *Luxury Sport*, and *Virtual Cockpit*.
- **The Nothing OS Collection:** Aesthetic dot-matrix and concentric layouts including *Nothing Dot Matrix*, *Nothing Concentric*, *Nothing Striped*, and *Nothing Red Strike*.

- **Astro & Vintage:** *Astronomy Orbit* (tracking smooth planetary paths) and *Vintage Gold* for an elegant luxury appearance.

### 2️⃣ 8 Ultra-Fluid Live Wallpapers
Beyond a simple dark background, FOLY AUTO introduces dynamic, battery-efficient live canvas textures:
- **Piano Pitch Black:** Pure absolute black optimized for maximum contrast and zero night-driving glare.
- **Carbon Fiber Texture:** High-fidelity weave texture providing a premium motorsport cabin feel.
- **Smart Interactive Map:** A beautiful pseudo-navigational live canvas displaying active digital radars, compass headings, altitude indicators, and coordinate grids mimicking a cockpit dashboard.
- **Cosmic Nebula Live:** Deep-space dust particles that gently dim and glow to mimic a clear night sky.
- **Golden Waves & Frosted Aurora:** Liquid-smooth gradients and premium flows that cascade gently on the screen borders.
- **Calm Matrix Rain & Dynamic Photon:** Advanced algorithmic digital rain and quantum waves reflecting engine physics.

### 3️⃣ 3D Layout Alignment & Dynamic Scaling
- **Widescreen & Split-Screen Optimization:** Fully compatible with modern Android Auto *Coolwalk* and media split layouts. Shift the clock placement up to `300px` horizontally (Left ⟷ Right) and `200px` vertically (Up ⟷ Down) to clear navigation maps.
- **Micro-Scaling:** Dynamically resize the watch dial pixel-by-pixel to perfectly complement your specific head unit dimensions.
- **Custom Gallery Engine:** Import any picture from your phone, crop it into a perfect circle locally, and map it as a custom high-contrast watch dial overlay.

### 4️⃣ Dynamic Emblem Branding & Typography Accent
- **Bespoke Emblems:** Type your own custom text or showcase a transparent brand logo (e.g., car manufacturer badge) right on the clock dial.
- **Typographic Engine:** Switch between 7 distinct premium font families (Sans, Serif, Monospace, Condensed, Sans-Black, Serif Italic, and Cursive Script) with absolute independent scaling and precise coordinate offsets.

### 5️⃣ Smooth Mechanics & Adaptive Themes
- **Smooth Sweep Seconds:** Optional ultra-smooth fluid ticking mechanism simulating luxury automatic mechanical movements (runs at an optimized 30 FPS / 33ms interval).
- **Dual Calendar Layout:** Concurrent render support for both Hijri and Gregorian dates with independent opacity controls.
- **Contrast Guard System:** Smart adaptive canvas rendering. The app dynamically monitors background choices—shifting indicators and watch needles to deep contrasting shades on bright backgrounds, ensuring 100% legibility under direct sunlight.
- **Native Adaptive Themes:** Full native support for **Light Mode** & **Dark Mode** on the companion phone control panel.

## 🚀 Sideloading & Installation Guide

Since premium automotive utilities built independently run outside the standard Google Play Store parameters, follow these 3 precise steps to enable the app on your dashboard:

1. **Enable Android Auto Developer Settings:**
   Open the Android Auto app settings on your smartphone. Scroll down to **Version**, and tap it rapidly 10 times until a pop-up appears stating that Developer Mode is active.
2. **Expose Unknown Sources:**
   Tap the three vertical dots on the top right corner to access **Developer Settings**. Scroll down to locate **Unknown Sources** and toggle the checkbox to active.
3. **Reconnect & Drive:**
   Unplug your smartphone's USB cable from the car and reconnect it. The dynamic **FOLY AUTO** icon will instantly appear inside your vehicle's app drawer launcher.

*Note: On ultra-strict system ROMs, sideloading the package via utilities like `KingInstaller` will disguise the package natively as an official Play Store bundle to bypass strict system blacklists.*
